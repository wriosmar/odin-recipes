Denver Cut

Description

A description about the Denver Cut.

Ingredients

  • Denver Cut
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cover the Denver Cut with salt and pepper on sides.
  2. Add a bit of butter to a pan on medium heat.
  3. Once hot, sear each side for five minutes.
  4. Add butter and garlic on top of last side and cover pan with lid.
  5. Let rest, then cut into slices.
  6. Enjoy with a side of mashed potatoes and asparagus.
